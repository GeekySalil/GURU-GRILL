About Us

Welcome to Guru Grill, a pure vegetarian fast food destination in the heart of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Known for our irresistible flavors, fresh ingredients, and perfect balance of taste and hygiene, we have become a favorite spot for food lovers across the city. At Guru Grill, we take pride in serving 100% pure vegetarian delicacies that satisfy both your cravings and your conscience. Our menu is designed to bring you the best of fast food while staying true to our commitment to quality and freshness.

Our Specialties

We are best known for our signature Chaap, Momos, and Paneer Tikkas, each prepared with authentic recipes, aromatic spices, and utmost care. These dishes have earned us the love and trust of countless customers who keep coming back for the unforgettable taste. Dine-in, Takeaway & Events Whether you want to enjoy a cozy dine-in experience, grab a quick takeaway, or plan a bulk order for events and celebrations, Guru Grill has you covered. We also provide exclusive discounts and deals on group or party orders, making us the perfect partner for your special occasions.
